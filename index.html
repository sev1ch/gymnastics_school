About us

At the Shining Gymnastics School, classes are held in comfortable equipped halls. An experienced coach selects loads based on the age and individual abilities of children. Trainings are held in 2 directions of gymnastics - artistic and aesthetic.

  • Result without harm to health

  • Own training base

  • Over 30 years of experience

What makes us the best?

  • 1

    Our coaches are masters of sports with specialized education, who improve their skills at seminars of international level specialists.

  • 2

    There is always a doctor present during training. Also, 2 times a year, children undergo a full medical examination.

  • 3

    Throughout the year, open lessons, photo and video projects for our gymnasts, a New Year's holiday and master classes with world rhythmic gymnastics stars are held.

  • 4

    Psychologists and nutritionists work with us to ensure the highest results.

  • 5

    A training camp is a long cycle of additional training sessions with the aim of improving athletic skills in a short period of time.

  • 6

    This makes it possible for all our gymnasts to take part in qualifying competitions and perform sports categories.
